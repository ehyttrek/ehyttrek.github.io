My road to becoming an HTML developer

What I want to learn

I want to learn how to better code. How to better integrate the 'ingredients' of the web as we know it today: HTML, CSS, JavaScript and serverside scripting (such as PHP).

I also want to improve my skills in teaching these skills to others. Or at least the skills that I have mastered so far (or more precisely: of which I believe I have mastered them).

Last but not least, I am interested in 'the big picture'. How does it all fit together? How is coding a part of our current culture? Is it a 'language' our kids should learn in school? Will it really make a difference once 'the machines' take over? Will it not be more valuable to focus on the human skills, the social skills, the soft skills, the interpersonal communication skills?
